about us

Nations Hospital has been a trusted name in Sri Lankan health care for more than 4 decades. Since our foundation in 1960, we have built a reputation for regional leadership in medical excellence and innovation, based on a simple philosophy: that improving the health of our community should be driven by passion as well as compassion. We offer 700 beds – including 80 in our critical care department – across a range of spacious, modern rooms. We offer the best consultants, specialists and employees, all of whom are dedicated to providing exceptional clinical outcomes and utmost customer satisfaction.

C

Children’s specialist


Nations Hospital offers a world-class paediatric unit with a wide array of services to address children’s changing healthcare needs, from infancy to adolescence. Dedicated to the nurturing care of children, we provide excellence in medical, surgical and rehabilitative care, with paediatricians who are sensitive to children’s needs and responsive to parental concerns.The paediatric unit is equipped with the latest in technology to offer the best possible treatment, from neonatal intensive care to general paediatrics.

D

Dermatologists


The Nations ‘Enhance’ dermatology and cosmetic care clinic is a facility that has been at the forefront of cosmetic care in the country. Within our sophisticated center, we have the power to revitalize and rejuvenate you and show you a new side to life. Our extensive array of dermatological and cosmetic procedures are carried out by expert consultants who focus on aesthetics and minimally invasive methods in order to ensure comfort and reliability.

N

Neurologists


We are equipped with the latest technology to undertake complex neurosurgery, including procedures to treat conditions causing stroke and hydrocephalus, and to remove brain tumours. Beyond successful treatment, we empower our customers by providing a multidisciplinary approach specially designed to address individual needs. Based on your specialist’s recommendation, Durdans Hospital provides neuro-rehabilitation services that include occupational therapy, physiotherapy and speech therapy





Board of Directors

Our business model is a customer centric one that is aligned with the needs to establish centres of excellence in all our areas of expertise within the framework of sustainability development. In our journey forward, we will create a paradigm shift in how we approach healthcare, reposition ourselves to empower patients, educate and engage them in their own health management thereby creating better value for their money spent.

The board of Directors is responsible for ensuring the stability and resilience of the company while overseeing its growth and success within a dynamic environment. It is engaged in approving, and monitoring the execution of the overall business strategy, strategic objectives, corporate values and policies.
