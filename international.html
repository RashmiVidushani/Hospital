International Patient Care Centre


Nations international patient care center is well known for offering World Class Healthcare in Sri Lanka. International Patient Centre is a dedicated centre at Nations Hospitals designed to provide personalized, quality care for international patients travelling to Sri Lanka from overseas. We ensure that you are well looked after by our team of trained health professionals.




Our medical services

Nations Hospitals is a 1500-bed multi-specialty tertiary care hospital in Sri Lanka that spreads over 990,000 square feet with 9 acres of beautifully landscaped garden. We offer state-of-the-art facilities that are complemented by cutting-edge technology and a well-experienced and trained staff, including some of Sri Lanka’s best specialists who have received professional training locally and/or overseas. Lanka Hospitals is the only private medical facility in Sri Lanka equipped with a helipad for air-ambulance services.

Blood Cancer Centre

We have a dedicated and fully equipped facility for all blood related cancer treatments, the only such treatment facility in Sri Lanka



Emergency Services

On call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, Nations Hospitals is possibly Sri Lanka’s best choice for treatment in a medical emergency, as we offer minimal waiting times and a dedicated unit of experienced doctors of all specialities of medicine.



Eye Clinic

We are the only private eye clinic in Sri Lanka to offer a complete range of services in one central location, led by local and foreign consultant ophthalmologists with highly trained nurses



Gastroenterology

This is a self-contained unit specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of gastro-intestinal, hepatology disorders, led by a team of consultant gastroenterologists and surgeons.
