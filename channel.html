General Care

Our ACLS and ATLS-certified physicians treat a wide range of emergencies, from cardiac attacks to strokes, sepsis, and injuries. Cardiovascular Life Support Ambulances are available at the center, and they are well-equipped with life-saving equipment. The squad insists on delivering treatment to patients from the moment they are summoned, when they begin advising families on how to care for patients long before they arrive. If they arrive, the patient receives all necessary treatment and en route to the hospital for continued specialty care. This increases the likelihood of a patient's recovery.

Cancer Care

Each customer receives loving, supportive, and individualized assistance from the Cancer Care Services team. In order to minimize the pain caused by cancer, our certified social workers eagerly match cancer patients, families, and survivors with services to address their financial, mental, and social needs. These programs are open to anybody in Tarrant County who lives there, regardless of age, diagnosis, or recovery level.

Heart Care

Cardiology is the study and management of cardiac and blood vessel problems. The discipline covers medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital cardiac abnormalities, coronary artery disease, heart attack, valvular heart disease, and electrophysiology. The cardiology staff has extensive experience diagnosing and treating coronary diseases. In order to ensure an effective diagnosis of the problem, the cardiologist can recommend some testing and perform some procedures, such as cardiac catheterizations, angioplasty, or the insertion of a pacemaker.

Eye Care

Ophthalmology is the branch of medicine that deals with disorders of the eyes. Patients of all ages may receive intensive diagnosis and treatment for illnesses such as glaucoma, cataracts, age-related macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and more. The staff delivers specialist consultation, precise and timely diagnosis, and treatment for a variety of eye disorders. The department is well-equipped to provide a full range of surgical and medical eye care for patients of all ages, including vision stabilization, security, reconstruction, and enhancement. A highly qualified team of ophthalmologists also provides high-quality eye care facilities.
