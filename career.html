Career Centre


We provide our people with every opportunity to progress their careers. Several key areas, such as Medical, Nursing, Laboratory, Front Office and Maintenance, already have in place specific career progression plans for employees. We have also developed a competency-based framework that focuses on building the competencies of our people.




Looking for a career in healthcare but your expertise is not advertised? Send your CV with a covering letter and we will contact you when a position of your discipline arises.

Write the position for which you are applying at the top left-hand corner of the envelope or on the subject line of the e-mail.
